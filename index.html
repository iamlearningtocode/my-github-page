Photo: Elsword at her first print gallery opening in 2002.
Elsword is a graphics designer based in Toronto, Ontario.
In 2002, Elsword founded Xeuhara Designs, a design agency.

Elsword and Xeuhara Designs create visual concepts, by hand or using computer software, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers.

Elsword and Xeuhara Designs develop the overall layout and production design for products, products packagings, advertisements, brochures, magazines, and corporate reports.

Elsword holds a bachelor degree in graphic design.
